Who?

This is a resource for Erli, the Romani dialect spoken by the community of Fakulteta. The Erli have been part of Sofia for several centuries. The name Erli comes from the Turkish word yerli, meaning local, belonging to a place.

Picture placeholder, 4 by 5

I am Jakob. Since I work at HESED and spend a few hours in Fakulteta nearly every day, I wanted to learn Erli. There are few existing resources, so I made this page.
