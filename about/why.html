Why?

The site collects vocabulary, example sentences, short video recordings, and downloadable Anki decks across ten introductory lessons. Materials draw on existing scholarly references, including Kiril Kostov's grammar of Sofia-Erli, alongside fresh recordings made with community speakers in neighbourhoods such as Fakulteta. Each lesson is built around an everyday topic: greetings, family life, food and the market, visits to the doctor, work, school, holidays, and storytelling.

The project is non-commercial and open access. The site is deliberately low-tech: plain HTML, no tracking, no scripts, no heavy media, so it loads quickly on any device including older basic phones with limited bandwidth. Contributions, corrections, and additional recordings from speakers and researchers are welcome.
